My top 3 TV shows of all time

Money Heist

Money Heist is an exhilirating TV series that follows a group of specialized robbers attempting the most perfect heist in Spanish history: Stealing 2.4 billion euros from Royal Mint of Spain. The show is intricate plot, relatable characters, and high-stakes action make it a thrilling watch.

Prison Break

It revolves around a genius structural engineer, Michael Scofield, who meticulously plan to liberate his brother, Lincoln Burrows, from prison. Lincoln is awaiting execution for allegedly murdering the Vice President's brother. The show is intricate plotlines, high-stakes escapes, and unforgettable characters make it a thrilling watch.

Suits

A gripping legal drama, masterfully blends drama with humor. Amiid intense storylines and high-stakes legal battles, the show delivers funny moments that stand out. From Harvey's failed flirting attempts to satirical interpretations of Mike and Harvey's bromance, Suits keeps viewers entertained season after season.
